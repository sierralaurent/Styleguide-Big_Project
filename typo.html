For typography, the New Habits Web-App wanted a clean, welcoming typeface that will continually remind the users that this app is a safe space.

Two typefaces were chosen, one Serif for titles and major heading, and one sans-serif for text and small headings. These typefaces have been found to be clean, appealing and had continuous legibility throughout it’s font-weight and size changes.
