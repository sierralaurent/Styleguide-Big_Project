The New Habits Web-App is for people who have been struggling with substance misuse, and think they may be nearing the point of no return. This app has been designed tho help those, by including a short quiz to help users realize the severity of their misuse. It also includes points to help users understand that substance misuse not only effects them, but their family and friends. New Habits end goal is to help it’s users and get them into their recovery journey, by providing a informational web-app as their zero-judgment safe zone.

The New-Habits web app will ask users a series of questions, narrowing down their substance misuse to a level of severe to not-severe and how they can seek help and support from a trained professional. Questions include asking if they have noticed any financial problems, health concerns and any troubles in personal and romantic relationships. When users answer these questions, they will then open their eyes and realize how their substance misuse is effecting themselves and others. After completion of the mini-quiz, the Web-app will then provide resources for the user to seek out help from trained professionals, and where they can learn more about substance addiction and misuse.
