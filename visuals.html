For Visuals, the New Habits Web-App wanted to have
clean and calming visuals to match the overall theme, not disturbing the users with bright colors and an overwhelming amount of items to look at, and letting them know this is a safe space to be themselves.

In the end purple and peach were found to be the best colors to meet the expectations of clean and calming. As well as continuing the use of the logo’s “Humphrey the Whale” to use as graphics when reaching certain celebration or saddening points in the app.
